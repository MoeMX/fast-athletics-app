Fundamental And Sound Training

FAST Athletics

We develop youth ages 5–18 through supervised track and field coaching, competition, academic encouragement, and character development — making participation accessible to all families.

Mission

Develop youth through track & field, academic encouragement, and character — with access for every family regardless of ability to pay.

Vision

Set the standard for excellence — athletes who train fast, dominate with integrity, and achieve in the classroom and on the track.
